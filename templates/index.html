About the Department

The Department of Public Administration and Local Government at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, is a beacon of excellence, committed to shaping the next generation of public sector leaders and innovators.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in public administration and local government studies, recognized globally for its innovative research, impactful community engagement, and the production of highly competent and ethical professionals.

Our Mission

To equip students with profound theoretical knowledge and practical skills in public administration, local government, and policy formulation, fostering critical thinking, research capabilities, and a commitment to public service and sustainable development.

---

Our Academic Programmes

We offer a range of distinguished programs designed to cater to diverse academic and career aspirations in public service.

B.Sc. Public Administration

Our undergraduate program provides a robust foundation in governance, public policy, human resource management, and local government administration, preparing students for entry-level public sector roles.

M.Sc. Public Administration

An advanced degree program focusing on specialized areas of public management, policy analysis, and leadership development, designed for professionals seeking to enhance their expertise and career progression.

Ph.D. Public Administration

Our doctoral program is tailored for aspiring researchers, academics, and high-level policy experts, fostering advanced research skills and contributing new knowledge to the field of public administration.
